Welcome to the Akan Name Generator
Would you like to know what your name would have been if you were a Ghanian?

This application takes your birthday and calculates the day of the week you were born.

Depending on your gender it gives you your Akan Name.

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
